# Sanitized historical aggregate

- Design: 32 frozen samples, four sectors, four systems, raw/controlled paired prompts.
- Execution: 32/32 terminal successful outputs after preserving setup failures and same-prompt retries; first-attempt success was 27/32.
- Evidence audit: 160 decision-changing claims, five per report. 53/160 were fully or partially supported; 107/160 were unsupported, contradicted or unverifiable.
- Anonymous scoring: the Industry Report Agent averaged 91.20/100 on raw product usability and 87.48/100 on controlled research-decision quality.
- Timing: successful runs totalled 10.78 hours; median successful runtime was 9.1 minutes.

Limitations: this was an end-to-end product comparison. Models, context budgets and search backends differed, so score gaps are not a pure Agent-architecture causal estimate. No manual-research time baseline was collected.
